Format

This is a virtual / live hybrid course. This means there will be trainees performing procedures on patients at the host centre being trained by local trainers and virtual trainers (online). We have tried hard to make this experience as close to being in the room as possible.

All links to join are available on the gieqs.com site (details below). Remote participants can follow via Zoom (low quality video but allows audio interaction) and/or a high quality stream on gieqs.com (video and audio with no interaction).

Please be aware there will be a small difference in the timing (around 20 seconds) of the two streams. Therefore it is advised to watch one stream or the other.

Interaction is also possible via the Slido web app where you can respond to questions, polls and ask your own questions at any time (link on gieqs.com).

Please mute your microphone when not interacting on the Zoom stream.

Browser Requirements

Following this course requires Google Chrome or Apple Safari browser. Internet Explorer will not work. Both these browsers are freely available. No responsibility will be taken by the organising committee if you cannot follow and are not using either of the named internet browsers.

How to join the course

Start by going to gieqs.com and logging in.

On the day of the course you will see your course name highlighted in the navigation bar at the top of the homepage [or accessible from the menu on a mobile device] (see below).

From this menu you can:

  • Watch the high definition live stream
  • Access the Zoom Session for Audio and low quality video interaction
  • Access the Slido app for asking questions using your SmartPhone or from your computer
  • Access the live programme and check upcoming and current sessions

Technical Assistance

If you need technical assistance please first check your internet connection and / or wifi connection.

If you have locked out your account (perhaps logged in on multiple devices or at multiple locations simultaneously) please contact us. To prevent this happening again logout from one device before you login with another. Your account and subscription allows login from as many devices as you wish but not simultaneously.

For any other technical issues please contact us.

Feedback

We greatly appreciate any feedback you may have, positive or negative.

We will a link with you after the course. Please take the time to let us know what went well and what could be done better next time.

Each completed feedback gives the chance to receieve a FREE subscription to a future GIEQs virtual / hybrid course in the future.
