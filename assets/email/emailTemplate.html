Ghent International Endoscopy Quality Symposium

Edition I, Wed 8 / Thurs 9 October 2020

Ghent, Belgium

Dear Colleague,

We are delighted to invite you to Ghent (Belgium) for the first edition of the Ghent International Endoscopy Quality Symposium (GIEQs), scheduled for October 7-8, 2020.

Pronounced 'geeks'1, GIEQs is a symposium dedicated to everyday techniques in Endoscopy. We believe quality in endoscopy is applying technical competence and published evidence to everything from a diagnostic gastroscopy to a circumferential ESD.

The GIEQs faculty will comprise renowned international experts responsible for driving quality in Endoscopy, including Professor Michael Bourke, Associate Professor Steven Heitman, Professor Raf Bisschops and Drs Roland Valori and John Anderson.

The majority of established endoscopy symposia focus on complex techniques performed in tertiary centres. GIEQs will focus on the opposite end of the spectrum. By holding a 2-day international symposium with a world-class faculty, focused on everyday techniques in Endoscopy, we hope to start a renewed drive towards quality in all endoscopic procedures in Belgium and throughout Europe.

You can view our mission statement for more information.

GIEQs is aimed at everyone practising endoscopy; there will be specific sessions for physicians, trainees and a separate nursing symposium for endoscopy nurses a first in Belgium!.
